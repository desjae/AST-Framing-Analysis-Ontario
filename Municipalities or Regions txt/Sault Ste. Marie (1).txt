Active Transportation
Active transportation encompasses all forms of human-powered travel including walking, cycling,
rollerblading, skiing, and canoeing.
The City prioritizes the development of safe and enjoyable active transportation routes to encourage
healthy lifestyles, decrease automobile dependency, and increase recreational tourism opportunities.
